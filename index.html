About Me

Hi, I'm Felipe Cañas Trincado, a Chilean Game Design student and Generalist. I started making and publishing my own games when I was 13 Years old, building passion for designing and creating entertainment through videogames. At 20 years old, I started studying in the Virtual Reality and Digital Games Engineering Program from Universidad Bernardo O'Higgins, and once I decided Making games is my life passion, I didn't stop there. I joined various events, game jams, and conferences about videogames, really getting into the medium, and continuing to expand on knowledge and experience. Here you can visit my greatest projects to date. If you like something, feel free to contact me so we can have some business talk.
